## Use Case 002 - Shoot Cannon

- **Primary Actor:** Player
- **Description:** The player aims and fires the cannon using the mouse.
- **Preconditions:** The player has clicked start and is in the main game loop. Canvas is loaded, timer is running.
- **Main Success Scenario:**
  1. Player clicks a location on the Canvas.
  2. The cannon's barrel rotates to point toward the click location.
  3. The cannonball is fired in a straight line in that direction.
    - 3a. The game plays a firing sound (`cannon_fire.wav`).
  4. The shot counter increments: `+=1`.
  5. Extension Point: cannonball makes contact.
- **Extensions:**
  - **5a. Hit Target:** The cannonball makes contact with a target. `target_hit.wav` plays. Three seconds are added to the timer, score increases by 1.
  - **5b. Hit Blocker:** The cannonball makes contact with a blocker. `blocker_hit.wav` plays. Three seconds are removed from the timer.
  - **5c. Miss Target:** The cannonball makes contact with a wall, and despawns.
